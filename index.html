About Us

Bring the whole family to this great place with lots of room for fun.we have 1200 squarefeet building with ground and first floor.its at the end of our village and right next to cauvery canal.

Things you can expect being at our stay.

Exclusive building to you, don’t need to share with anybody. Building consistes ground and first floor Ground floor has 2 bedrooms and common restroom with living room and kitchen. First floor has one large bedroom with 2 king beds and attached restroom and also very spacious party cum dining hall. Clean and hygiene indoor space with comfy beds and linens. Spacious party hall cum dining hall exposed to nature and having canal view. Authentic mandya style home made food at extra cost. three acre farm land with complete protective green mesh around the property.

Outside activities

Indoor games carom, chess Outdoor games like cricket, shuttle cock. Cycling on the banks of the canal. 3 kids cycles,2 adult cycles and electric jeep and toy guns are available for kids to play. Camp fire under a mango tree and barbeque can be arranged.

Places around our farmstay

Mysore—world renowned heritage city you can visit-palace,chamundi hills,zoo etc Krs back water-incredible view of krs dam back water. French rock(kunti betta)—short and sweet mountain trekking spot. Gowthama kshetra— highland surrounded by Cauvery river,place gowthama rishi did tapas. Sri rangapatna—it was capital city of tipu sultan and famous temple of sriranganathaswamy temple.

We provide services of guiding you in kuntibetta trekking spot on a chargeable basis
